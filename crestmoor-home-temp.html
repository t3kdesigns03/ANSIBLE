About the Course

Nine Challenging Holes

Our well-maintained nine-hole layout offers three tee sets to accommodate every skill level – from beginners to seasoned players.

Handicap & Ratings

Member of the Iowa Golf Association with official GHIN handicap system. Rated course with established slope and rating.

Proud Home of Champions

2013 Creston/OM Boys 3A State Champions • 2018 Girls 2nd Place State Finals

Book Your Tee Time

Ladies Day Wednesdays at Noon • Men's Day Thursdays at Noon – Special Rates!

Amenities & Facilities

Driving Range

Open to the public with grass tees and covered stalls. Perfect your swing before your round.

Pro Shop

Basic golf supplies, gloves, balls, apparel, and friendly advice from our staff.

Snack Bar

Grab a cold drink, hot dog, or sandwich between nines. Outdoor seating available.

Cart Rentals

Gas carts available for 9 or 18 holes. Pull carts also offered.

Programs & Events

Junior Golf Program (Ages 7–16)

Weekly lessons, etiquette training, and participation in traveling tournaments throughout southwest Iowa. Building tomorrow's golfers today!

Leagues & Tournaments

Men's, Ladies', Couples', and Senior leagues. Annual club championship and fun scrambles throughout the season.
